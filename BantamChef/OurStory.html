Our Story

More than just a restaurant. A hometown tradition built on fresh food, friendly faces, and serving the Marion community with pride.

A Marion Tradition

For generations, Bantam Chef has been a place where neighbors gather, families share meals, and travelers discover one of Marion's favorite hometown restaurants. From hearty breakfasts to juicy burgers, fresh seafood, and delicious gyros, every meal is prepared with care and served with a smile.

Known for its welcoming atmosphere and friendly staff, Bantam Chef has built its reputation on consistency, quality, and treating every guest like family. Whether you're a longtime local or just passing through, you'll find great food and genuine hospitality waiting for you.

Today, under new ownership, Bantam Chef continues that tradition while introducing new menu items and maintaining the quality and service that generations of customers have come to expect.

More Than Great Food

Bantam Chef has become part of everyday life in Marion. Families gather around the table after church, coworkers stop in for lunch, and travelers discover a local favorite while passing through town.

From hearty breakfasts that start the morning right to satisfying dinners after a long day, every guest is welcomed with genuine hospitality and food prepared with care.

Our goal has always been simple: serve delicious food, provide friendly service, and create an atmosphere where everyone feels at home.
