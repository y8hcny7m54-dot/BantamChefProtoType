Welcome to Bantam Chef

Since 1971, Bantam Chef has proudly served Marion and the surrounding communities with delicious comfort food, generous portions and friendly service. Whether you're stopping in for breakfast before work, grabbing lunch with friends or bringing the family for dinner, you'll always find fresh food and welcoming smiles.
