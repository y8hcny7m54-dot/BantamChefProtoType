A Marion Tradition

Bantam Chef has served the Marion community since 1971. What makes Bantam Chef unique isn't just the food. It's the atmosphere. Colorful window paintings. Friendly staff. Fresh burgers. Southern breakfasts. Families gathering around tables they've visited for decades.

Whether you're stopping by for breakfast before work or bringing the family for dinner, you'll find the same welcoming hometown experience every time.
